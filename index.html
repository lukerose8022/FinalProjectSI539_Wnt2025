Klaas Arjans Aardema b. 11/24/1867 Boer, Friesland
d. 6/14/1951

Klaas immigrated from the Netherlands and became a cabinet-maker in Grand Rapids, Michigan. He lived to age 83 and was father to Elizabeth Aardema.

Turnisje "Tillie" Visser b. 12/31/1874 Friesland
d. 7/25/1927

Tillie immigrated from Friesland and lived as a homesteader near Evart, Michigan. She died at 52 from a ruptured appendix.
